Our Story

Built to help
businesses hold.

TakeHold Media exists because too many good businesses get lost online — not because they don't have something to offer, but because nobody helped them show up.

Who We Are

A lean agency that moves like a team, not a committee.

TakeHold Media is a digital marketing agency operating as a DBA of TMB Holdings. We work with local and growing businesses that need more than a pretty website — they need a real digital presence that generates leads, builds trust, and keeps working long after launch.

We're small by design. That means the people you talk to are the people doing the work. No account managers playing telephone between you and a developer overseas. Just direct, accountable, high-quality execution.

Our name comes from the anchor — a tool that doesn't just sit there, it holds. That's what we build for your brand: a foundation that holds under pressure and lets everything else grow from it.

What We Believe

Four things we won't compromise on

Foundation first

We don't layer campaigns on a broken foundation. Identity, website, and positioning come first — then we build growth on top of something solid.

Radical transparency

You'll always know what we're working on, what it costs, and what it's producing. No mystery metrics, no smoke and mirrors.

Built, not assembled

We build from scratch. No page builders, no template shops, no outsourcing. If your name is on it, so is ours — and we take that seriously.

Long-term thinking

We're not interested in quick wins that fade. We'd rather build something that still works for you three years from now than spike your numbers for a month.

Our Approach

Anchor. Grow. Hold.

Most agencies want to run ads on day one. We slow down first. If your brand is unclear, your website is slow, or your positioning is muddled — no amount of ad spend will fix that.

So we anchor first: build a clear brand identity, a fast site, and a message that actually connects. Once that foundation is solid, we layer on SEO, social, and campaigns that compound over time.

The result isn't a quick spike. It's a business that holds its position in the market and grows from a stable base — which is exactly what the name is about.

01 Anchor

Build a brand and digital foundation that's clear, fast, and positioned to compete. Identity, website, messaging.

02 Grow

Layer on SEO, social content, and targeted campaigns that bring the right audience to what you've built.

03 Hold

Stay in. Monitor, optimize, and adapt as the market shifts. Your brand keeps its position while you focus on your business.
